# Deployment

## Prerequisites

- MicroK8s installed
- kubectl configured

## Deployment Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare Environment">
    Prepare your environment for deployment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy Components">
    Deploy the infrastructure components.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Deployment">
    Verify all components are running.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Post-Deployment

After deployment, verify your cluster:

```bash
# Verification commands
kubectl get nodes
kubectl get pods -A
```
